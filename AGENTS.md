# Blog tone

Before editing any `.mdx` under `src/content/blog/`, read `src/content/blog/TONE.md`. It defines the voice conventions for posts on this site — specific patterns to avoid (bolded-punchline headlines, sweeping connectors, uninterrogated jargon) and patterns to use (numbers-first claims, specific proper nouns, inline hedging of extrapolations). The reference post for the voice is `crux-windows.mdx`.
